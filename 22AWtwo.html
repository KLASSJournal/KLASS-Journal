Tons of new sets have been released this season with 26 new City sets, 8 new Star Wars sets, the Lego Ideas Medieval Blacksmith and many others. Riding on the heels of the historic 501st Battle pack from last year, Lego Star Wars has introduced a brand new scale for their iconic ships. What could by far be the biggest change this year is Lego‚Äôs unexpected shift to a new buildable road plate system.
