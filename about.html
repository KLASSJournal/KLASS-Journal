ABOUT US

A blog built, written and run by KLASS students for other students.



WHO

We are a group of Alice Smith students who are truly passionate about something we love, and wanted a platform to express ourselves from.

WHAT

Articles ranging from literature to law, maths to music, sports to science and even the personal side of life at Alice Smith. We're also a place to promote student-led initiatives, competitions and clubs.

WHY

Our goal is to provide all KLASS students with the opportunity to demonstrate their uniqueness to the rest of the KLASS community, setting them apart from a sea of 800 or so EP students by showing what it is that drives them forward everyday.

CONTRIBUTE

See our contribute page to propose your idea to us. All articles are moderated by our editors and the school. Students are reminded that in writing for this blog they will effectively act as online ambassadors for The Alice Smith School, and that their content may also be seen by people outside of the school community. As such, your content should reflect the core values of Alice Smith.
